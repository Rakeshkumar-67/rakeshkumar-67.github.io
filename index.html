About Me

CFD and thermal-fluid engineer with 7+ years of simulation experience across turbomachinery, rotorcraft, and renewable energy systems.

I specialize in high-fidelity transient CFD analysis, development of User-Defined Functions (UDFs) for complex kinematics, and HPC-based parallel simulations. Currently, as a Postdoctoral Fellow at IIT Bombay, I focus on the aerodynamic optimization of cyclorotors and tandem rotor systems.
